Volunteering

I had the opportunity to volunteer with Teen Driving Solutions, a dedicated organization focused on improving teen driving safety through education and community involvement. Volunteering here allowed me to engage in meaningful activities that directly contribute to safer driving practices among teens. Additionally, I volunteered at my local church, participating in various community events that support safe driving awareness and local engagement.

What Did I Do?

During my time with Teen Driving Solutions, I collaborated with other volunteers to discuss and promote safe driving strategies. We organized workshops and participated in community outreach to raise awareness about the importance of responsible driving among teenagers. At my local church, I assisted with the annual HOA event held every December. My responsibilities included setting up canopies, helping carry items, and even assisting with animals for the petting cage they had. These experiences taught me the value of community service and the impact it can have on promoting safety and unity.
