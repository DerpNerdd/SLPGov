Action Plan

This Service Learning Project aims to accomplish three goals, but how will this project take on these difficult tasks? Navigate this page to learn about the action plan, intended outcome, and its implementation.

Connection to Research

Our action plan is grounded in thorough research and analysis of current teen driving challenges.

  • Identify the most significant risk factors for teen drivers based on recent studies.
  • Leverage data from successful programs to inform our strategies.
  • Utilize insights from experts in the field to shape our approach.

Intended Outcome

The intended outcome of our action plan is to significantly enhance teen driving safety and awareness.

  • Reduce the number of teen driving-related accidents and fatalities.
  • Increase awareness and adherence to safe driving practices among teens.
  • Foster a supportive community environment that prioritizes teen driving safety.

Implementation of Action Plan

The following steps outline how we will achieve our goals effectively and sustainably.

Goal 1: Increase Awareness
  • Conduct workshops and seminars in local schools and communities to educate teens about safe driving practices.
Goal 2: Improve Driver Education
  • Partner with local driving schools to offer comprehensive and affordable driver education programs.
Goal 3: Advocate for Policy Changes
  • Work with policymakers to draft and support legislation that strengthens teen driving safety laws.
